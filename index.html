Last updated: July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kuartet Card Game ("the App") handles information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect any personal information. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob – used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)

3. Advertising

The App displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may use advertising IDs and other information to provide relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, visit Google Ads Help.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App is rated for general audiences and does not contain content inappropriate for children.

5. Data Security

All game data (scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not operate any servers that store user data.
